Our Services

Cardio and Weight Training

Our facility is equipped with top-of-the-line cardio machines, such as treadmills, ellipticals, and stationary bikes, as well as a wide variety of weight training equipment, including free weights and resistance machines. Our certified personal trainers are available to help you design a workout plan that is tailored to your individual needs and goals.

Group Fitness Classes

We offer a wide variety of group fitness classes, led by experienced instructors, to help you get in shape and have fun. Our class offerings include yoga, Pilates, spin, cardio kickboxing, strength and conditioning, Zumba, and much more.

Personal Training

Our certified personal trainers are here to help you reach your fitness goals. Whether you are looking to lose weight, build muscle, or simply improve your overall health and fitness, our trainers will work with you to create a customized workout plan that is tailored to your individual needs.

Sauna and Steam Room

Relax and unwind after your workout in our sauna and steam room. These facilities are available to all members and are the perfect way to ease sore muscles and improve circulation.

Other Services

We also offer towel service, lockers and changing rooms, and a wide variety of supplements and fitness accessories available for purchase.
